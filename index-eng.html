SHELTER FOR ANIMAL PATITAS FELICES

We are an association of people who help all dogs that are unfortunately abandoned, neglected and rescued. or they were already born on the street. Non-profit shelter where a team of 40 volunteers work to give these dogs a second chance, since they deserve to be loved and have a home where they can be happy :)
